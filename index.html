DedoMouse

Control your computer mouse with your hands and a webcam!

The gesture based remote control of DedoMouse allows you to take a step back from your desk and reduce the time spent in uncomfortable positions.

Built on the excellent hand and finger tracking of Google MediaPipe.

Get DedoMouse

Useful for:

  • Presentations: show next slide or image
  • Simple browsing: for example, start a video
  • Reduced bacteria transfer: no need to touch an input device at an ATM or laboratory
  • Simple games: point and click adventures, visual novels, etc.
  • People who cannot or do not want to use a regular mouse (e.g. because of RSI)

Smartphone as Webcam

Tools like Iriun Webcam or iVCam allow to use a smartphone as webcam. Tip: Connect your smartphone via USB for better performance.

All Monitors

Freely configure the size of your monitor or even multiple monitors.

Intuitive Gestures

Use simple gestures, for example thumb up for scrolling.

Light and Dark

Choose a UI theme that suits your style.

Easy Setup

Immediately see and test changes to the settings.
